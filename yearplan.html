  • 5-year plan
Increase VCNC Supported Children

Our goal is to significantly expand the number of children we support. We will begin with a comprehensive needs assessment to identify gaps and areas for expansion. Based on this data, we will recruit and train additional staff and volunteers to manage the increased workload and establish partnerships with local organizations to enhance our reach and effectiveness.

Launch Customized VCNC Merchandise

To increase funding and promote our cause, we will develop and sell VCNC-branded merchandise such as t-shirts, bottles, and hoodies. We will design, produce, and market these items, with a strategic focus on generating revenue and raising awareness about our mission. Detailed planning will ensure successful execution and effective promotion.

Host Talent Show Events

We will organize talent show events to highlight the skills of our supported children. These events will include debates, dances, comedy, and theater performances. Our plan includes securing venues, managing logistics, promoting the events, and engaging with the community to maximize attendance and fundraising.

Establish a Training Hub

Aimed at providing valuable skills and knowledge, our training hub will offer workshops on essential topics and practical skills. We will organize sessions on challenges faced by the children, language skills, soft skills, and professional behaviors. This initiative will be supported by expert facilitators and tailored to address the specific needs of our beneficiaries.

Conduct Periodical Campaigns

We will run targeted campaigns to address specific needs such as meals, clothing, and other essentials. Each campaign will be carefully planned and executed to gather community support and raise funds for these critical items, ensuring that our children’s basic needs are consistently met.

Support Families with Business Grants

To enhance the financial stability of our beneficiaries' families, we will offer business grants. These grants will be designed to support small-scale entrepreneurship and economic empowerment. We will develop a grant application process, provide training, and monitor the impact to ensure successful implementation.

Offer After-School Support Services

We aim to provide comprehensive after-school support to help children apply for scholarships, secure internships, and access educational resources. Our services will include guidance and mentoring, ensuring that children have the support needed to advance their academic and career goals.

Provide Parenthood Training

Recognizing the importance of addressing root causes of poverty, we will offer training for parents to improve their parenting skills and financial management. This initiative will focus on creating a supportive environment for both children and their families, helping to break the cycle of poverty.

Preparation For a School

As part of our long-term vision, we will initiate preparations to build a school. This includes acquiring land, securing sponsors, and completing necessary documentation. Our goal is to provide a dedicated educational facility that will further our mission of supporting vulnerable children.
