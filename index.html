About Us

Our journey began with our founder's simple yet profound gesture of connecting with street children through the universal language of music. Playing guitar for emotional healing, visiting the families of those who had them, and listening to their stories, our founder laid the foundation for what would become a thriving community dedicated to the well-being of vulnerable children.

During the COVID-19 pandemic, the need to help these street children intensified even further. More children were exposed to poverty, more families fell unable to cater for their offspring, which all contributed to an increase of street children and school dropouts.

The founder reached out to like-minded youths to organize a Christmas day which took place on December 26, 2023, where 34 street children were hosted. This marked the birth of VCNC. The attendees were inspired by the dreams the kids had, and the desire to transform their lives. It was immediately concluded to take the most promising kids back to school, encourage them to go back to their families, and provide basic needs they faced regularly.

Mission

VCNC is dedicated to providing holistic support to young, vulnerable children facing adversity. Our mission is to empower these children by addressing challenges such as addiction to drugs (those already on street), limited access to education, and insufficient basic needs through creative initiatives, community engagement, and collaborative fundraising efforts. We strive to create a positive impact on their lives.

Vision

VCNC envisions a future where every vulnerable child not only overcomes adversity but thrives. Our vision is to build a society that prioritizes the well-being and education of these children, providing them with the support and opportunities needed for a brighter and more promising future.

Focal Areas of Support Program:

Health Insurance

VCNC ensures that every child under our care has access to quality healthcare through comprehensive health insurance coverage. By providing this essential support, we protect children from the financial burden of medical expenses and promote their overall well-being. Our commitment to health enables children to attend school regularly and participate fully in their communities.

School Materials

At VCNC, we understand that having the right tools is crucial for a successful education. We provide school materials such as uniforms, books, stationery, and other supplies to ensure that children have everything they need to succeed in their studies. This support helps create an equitable learning environment where every child can thrive.

School Fees

Education is a vital pathway to a brighter future, and VCNC is dedicated to making it accessible to all children. We cover school fees for children from vulnerable backgrounds, ensuring they have the opportunity to learn, grow, and reach their potential. By investing in education, we are helping to break the cycle of poverty and empower the next generation.

Hope to serve more

Looking forward, VCNC aims to expand our programs to provide holistic support for street children and other vulnerable populations. We plan to introduce initiatives focused on mental and emotional well-being, small business opportunities for family development, and after-school programs that foster creativity and personal growth. By broadening our scope, we hope to create a comprehensive support network that addresses all aspects of a child's life.
